Traditional Dances

Explore the vibrant dances of the Basotho people.

  • Seqoele: A dance that celebrates the harvest.
  • Mohobelo: A dance performed during weddings.
  • Letlama: A dance that tells stories of ancestors.

Traditional Instruments

Learn about the instruments that bring Basotho music to life.

  • Lesiba: A traditional string instrument.
  • Drums: Used in various ceremonies.
  • Harp: A melodic instrument often played during celebrations.
